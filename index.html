Instant command line

Summon Right AI anywhere with a single keystroke.

Hold your hotkey, start typing, and press Enter. Right AI captures the current page, sends your message to the selected model, and returns context-rich answers without breaking your flow.

  • Global shortcut: Use Ctrl + R (recommended) to wake the input from any tab.
  • Model switcher: choose GPT, Claude, Gemini, or custom copilots before sending.
  • Smart suggestions: inline tips keep the next action right at your fingertips.
  • Intent prediction: AI Quick Input can suggest what you may want to type or ask based on the current page. Toggle it in Settings → Zhipu Extension → Intent Prediction.

FAQ

Answers before you install.

Does Right AI support multiple AI providers?

Yes. Connect GPTs, Claude, Gemini, and self-hosted endpoints. Switch providers on the fly or run comparisons.

Is any data stored on your servers?

Your prompts and responses stay on the providers you authenticate with. You can optionally enable encrypted cloud sync for history.

Does it work outside of Chrome?

The extension installs on any Chromium browser, including Arc, Brave, and Edge. A Firefox version is in beta.
